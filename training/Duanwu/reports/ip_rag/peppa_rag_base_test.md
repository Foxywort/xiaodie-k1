=== "Peppa Pig effect" ===
Since 2019, it has been observed that children in the United States, where Peppa Pig had become an extremely popular programme, had been acquiring some British English pronunciation and vocabulary used in the show rather than their native American English.
For example, tomato and zebra are pronounced differently, words such as petrol (instead of gas) and biscuit (instead of cookie) are used, and comments such as "how clever", "oh dear" and "can I have a go?" picked up.
This phenomenon was not an issue for many parents; it trended with the hashtag "#PeppaEffect".
